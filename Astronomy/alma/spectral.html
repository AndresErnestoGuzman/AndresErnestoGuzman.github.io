Spectral resolution and Hanning smoothing in ALMA.

The autocorrelation signal from where the spectrum is derived is modified by a window function called a Hann window. We will denote \(w \leftrightharpoons W\), repectively, the Hann window function in the lag-autocorrelation domain and its Fourier transform, in the frequency domain. That is \begin{equation} \int_{-\infty}^\infty w(x) e^{-2\pi i x f} dx = W(f)~~.\label{eq-fourier} \end{equation}

Explicitely, Hann window and its Fourier transform are \begin{equation} w(x)=\begin{cases} \cos^2\left(\pi x\right)&|x|\le1/2\\ 0& \neg \end{cases} \leftrightharpoons \frac{\sin(\pi f)}{2\pi f(1-f^2)}=W(f)~~. \end{equation}
